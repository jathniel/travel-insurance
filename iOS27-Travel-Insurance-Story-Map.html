Three anchor moments to hit

  1. Steps 3–4: Travel app does real work — calls Coherent SPARK, returns real quote data — without opening. The iOS 27 differentiator, and Joel's core "beyond the app" moment.
  2. Step 4: Siri narrates while showing the card. Multimodal (voice + visual + tap or voice selection) — the antidote to Joel's "not chatbot style" ask.
  3. Step 6: Success stays in Siri. The app never opens at any point in the journey. Pure iOS 27 capability, deliberate demo choice.
